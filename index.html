Abstract

Conquer studies real-world quadruped coordination across tracking and fixed-point control scenarios. The demonstrations on this page collect single-robot and multi-robot deployments, highlighting how the system scales from one dog to coordinated teams while preserving stable, physical behavior on hardware.

The page is structured as a paper project site: concise project summary first, real-robot videos as the primary evidence, and citation metadata for integration with the final manuscript.
